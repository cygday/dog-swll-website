Support: Lifetime care for happy pets

At k9 pet zone, our support service keeps puppy families connected and confident long after adoption. We offer ongoing advice, follow-up care, and practical guidance so every dog and owner can enjoy a healthy, happy life together.

Get Support

What our support includes

Owner advice

We answer questions about nutrition, training, health, and puppy care by email or phone.

Follow-up check-ins

Regular support helps ensure your puppy transitions smoothly into a new home and routine.

Health reminders

We provide reminders for vaccinations, deworming, and wellness visits so your pup stays on track.

Behavior support

Get guidance on handling puppy behaviors and training challenges with calm, positive solutions.

Why support matters

Our support service is built so every family feels backed by experience and care. We help puppies thrive, owners feel confident, and long-term relationships grow strong.

  • Professional care advice after adoption
  • Simple follow-up tips for puppy health
  • Support for grooming, feeding, and training questions
  • Peace of mind from a caring kennel team
